Unbounded(Women Safety App)

"Unbounded Women Safety" is a user-friendly app focused on ensuring the safety of women. Key features include real-time location tracking, emergency SOS alerts, a virtual companion for check-ins, community watch for sharing safety information, safe route navigation, self-defense resources, 24/7 chat support, incident reporting, privacy controls, and an offline mode for areas with poor connectivity. The app aims to empower women with practical tools and a supportive community to enhance their personal safety.
